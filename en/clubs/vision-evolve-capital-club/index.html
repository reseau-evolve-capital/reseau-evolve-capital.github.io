Our Story

Origins

Founded in February 2024, Vision Evolve Capital (VEC) was born from a meaningful encounter between Louisia Minkala and Lionel Zoclancounon at a networking conference of BlackNetwork. Inspired by Lionel's presentation on investment clubs and their role in financial education, Louisia quickly embraced the idea and chose to take part. She became the club's first president, driven by a clear mission to boost financial literacy among women. Although open to all, VEC stands out within the network for its strong female representation, making it a unique and forward-thinking club. Under Louisia's leadership, VEC has become a space for collaborative learning, strategic investing, and financial empowerment—drawing from the shared experience of the Evolve Capital network.

Milestones

2024

Club launch. In less than eight months, VEC reached a portfolio valuation of €30,000.

Vision

By 2026, VEC aims to reach €100,000 in assets under management and foster greater collaboration among its members. The club aspires to become a benchmark for financial education among young professionals—especially women—through hands-on learning, peer support, and a structured approach to long-term investing.
