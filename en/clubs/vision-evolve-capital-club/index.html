Our Story

Origins

Founded in February 2024, Vision Evolve Capital was born from a pivotal encounter between Louisia Minkala and Lionel Zoclancounon at a networking conference. Captivated by Lionel’s presentation on investment clubs and their role in financial education, Louisia was immediately drawn to the idea and decided to join the initiative. Determined to take an active role, she later made the bold decision to become the club’s first president. Under her leadership, VEC has grown into a space where women can learn, invest, and build a strong financial future while benefiting from the experience of other clubs within the Evolve Capital network.
